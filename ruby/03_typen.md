* Nummern
  * `42` (Fixnum)
  * `3.14159` (Float)
* Booleans
  * `true`
  * `false`
* Strings
  * `"apple"`
  * `'banana'`
* Symbols
  * `:apple`

# Basis Typen (cont.)

* Regular Expressions
  * `/fo*/i`
* Arrays
  * `["apple", "banana"]`
* Ranges
  * `(1..10)`
* Hashes
  * `{:apple => 'red', :banana => 'yellow'}`
  * `{apple: 'red', banana: 'yellow'}`
